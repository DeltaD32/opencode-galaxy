# Imprint

> **Source**: BMW Group Density Design System
> For the most up-to-date guidelines, visit:
> https://density.bmwgroup.net/v11/content/voice-and-tone

---

An imprint is a brief summary of contact information and copyrights primarily required by German-speaking countries.

## 1. Specifications

The imprint should be easily recognizable, immediately available and always visible on the website or within an app. The title under which the required information can be retrieved is also generally to be titled as an "imprint". Variants such as "contact" or "information" should not be used. **An imprint is mandatory for all BMW Group applications.**

## 2. Mandatory Content

### General Information

The imprint contains the name, address and the legal form of the operator. The same requirements apply to the BMW Group as to BMW AG.

**English:**

> This website is maintained by Bayerische Motoren Werke Aktiengesellschaft (Petuelring 130, 80788 München).
>
> The legal representatives of BMW AG are the members of the Board of Management (Oliver Zipse (Chairman), Jochen Goller, Ilka Horstmeier, Nicolai Martin, Walter Mertl, Milan Nedeljković, Joachim Post).
> Chairman of supervisory board: Nicolas Peter

**German:**

> Diese Webseite wird von der Bayerische Motoren Werke Aktiengesellschaft (Petuelring 130, 80788 München) betrieben.
>
> Die BMW AG wird gesetzlich durch den Vorstand (Oliver Zipse (Vorsitzender), Jochen Goller, Ilka Horstmeier, Nicolai Martin, Walter Mertl, Milan Nedeljković, Joachim Post) vertreten.
> Vorsitzender des Aufsichtsrats: Nicolas Peter

### E-mail Address

It is sufficient to provide an e-mail address for direct contact. Use a product e-mail address and not a personalized one. This way, you can easily handle representations.

Automatic acknowledgment of receipt is allowed. However, the automatic response that refers to a contact form should not be used. If you are using a contact form, insert it directly into your imprint.

### Phone Number

Provide the operator's telephone number. As with the e-mail address, you should not provide a personal telephone number here, but a service hotline.

Without a phone number, you should offer a contact form that allows direct communication with you (such as a chat).

### Legal Information

Also mandatory is the registration in the commercial register, the registration number and the VAT identification number.

**English:**

> Commercial register Amtsgericht München Registergericht, HRB 42243.
>
> Value-added tax identification no.: DE129273398.

**German:**

> Sitz und Registergericht: München HRB 42243
>
> Umsatzsteueridentifikationsnummer: DE129273398
